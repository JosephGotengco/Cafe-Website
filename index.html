Why we Brew?
We think it is important to support our local community. That's why we source our coffee beans from local farmers / plantations. We have been brewing coffee since early 2018 and we are not slowing down anytime soon. We have employeed more than 2000 employees and have 74 stores across British Columbia. We plan to branch off into other provinces and later on, other countries. Our founder Joseph Gotengco was a web designer - the best web designer of the generation. He had a vision to create a webpage that will link everyone across the globe and show them the amazingness that is our coffee.
Our Locations
From the East Cost to West Cost, we have locations in all communities across Canada. We strategically built our locations not only to give coffee to the close-knit communities, but to source our beans from coffee plantations nearby. We value the people who help us make our coffee. Our headquarters are located in Vancouver, British Columbia.
